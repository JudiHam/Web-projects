my favorite makeup tricks

3.12.2019

complexion

  1. After applying concealer under the eyes, wait for it to dry a bit before blending.
    Also wait for it to dry before you set with powder. As a result the coverage will be way better.
  2. Keep your concealer in the fridge for a refreshing cool application in the morning.
  3. After adding bronzer and blush, blend everything with a soft brush and some translucent powder.
    This will make everything seamless!
  4. Spray on your favorite setting spray and lock the makeup in by going over everything with a makeup sponge.
  5. Add highlighter after setting spray for an extra kick. Set that in as well in with a final spray.

eyes

  1. If you like to keep it simple with eyeshadow, just add the same bronzer you applied to your face in your crease.
    This will bring some life to your eyes and bring the whole look together since the same tones have been added to different parts of the face.
  2. Spray some hairspray on a brow spoolie and use like a brow gel. It's cheap and easy. And your eyebrows won't budge, trust me.
  3. Spray some setting spray on your brush or finger before applying shimmery or metallic eyeshadows.

my holy grail beauty products

28.11.2019

These are my go-to items that I've either been repurchasing for years, or have recently added to my daily routine.

complexion

Maybelline Luminous and Smooth FitMe Foundation, 125
The only dewy foundation I dare use and it has surprisingly become my daily go-to!
Nyx Can't Stop Won't Stop Contour Concealer, Natural
One the best coverages of all concealers I've ever tried!
L'oreal Paris Infallible full coverage concealer, 328 Biscuit
I'm liking this the more I use it and I've started using it daily since I ran out of the Nyx one. I'll definitely purchase a second one when I run out.
Kicks Anti Dullness Correction Concealer
I'm still searching for the ultimate color corrector, but ever since I tried this one I can't go back to not using any corrector. It feels lightweight and thin, but it does a great job at correcting my dark circles.
Colourpop Loose Setting Powder, Translucent
It's the thinnest and featheriest texture and it sets so well! For comparison, I prefer it 10 times over the hyped Laura Mercier Translucent powder!
Pixi by Petra Gloss-y Gossamer Duo Highlighter in Subtle Suntrise
My go-to on the daily. This is what I reach for unless I have something new or I'm in a very particular makeup mood and crave for somethign else.
Physician's Formula Butter Bronzer
Heavenliest smell of coconuts and butteriness, blends and builds like a tropical dream...
Urban Decay All Nighter Setting Spray
This setting spray is pricy, but it's one of those hyped products that are hyped for a reason. I always feel super set with this spray and I would'nt trust any other to hold my glam when I need my makeup to last.

eyes

L'oreal Paris Waterproof Lash Paradise Mascara
Makes my lashes super long! I like to start application from the tips of my lashes and then build the base. This gives me super volume and lenght.
Anastasia Beverly Hills Brow Powder Duo, Ebony
For the easiest, fastest and fleekiest brows.

lips

Maybelline Color Sensational Lipliner, Velvet Beige
I've used this since 9th grade continuously. That's 5 years and countless lip liners. It's the perfect shade of brown for almost any look I do unless I use a really dark or bright red lipstick.
Maybelline Color Sensational Made for All Lipstick, Mauve for Me
The color is perfect, but I'd say it's more of a light brown than mauve. And the only thing I don't like is the smell.
Nyx Butter Gloss, Angel Food Cake
Again, heavenly fragrance is my weakness!

fragrance

Vibe Enhancer Body Mist by Missguided
This is my everyday body mist that people always compliment when I have it on.
Coconut Cloud Body Mist by Victoria's Secret
Coconutty but in a rarely fresh way.
Silver Strand Beach Body Mist by Hollister
It's a very unique scent inspired by mango and starfruit.
